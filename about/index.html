I’m Hanna Shibata, an independent Multidisciplinary Designer who is looking forward to be your next collaborator. I would love to bring in my design expertise from my experience of woking in-house and at agency firms.

I left Tokyo to pursue my graduate studies at Pratt Institute, and continued my design career in the United States since 2020.
